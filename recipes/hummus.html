Hummus

Description

Super easy hummus that only takes minutes. Great with veggies or pita chips. Really it's great with ANYTHING!

Ingredients

  • 1 can of chickpeas
  • 1 tablespoon of olive oil
  • 1 clove of garlic
  • 1/2 teaspoon of ground cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • 2 drops of seasame oil

Steps

  1. Blend chickpeas, lemon juice, olive oil, garlic, cumin, salt, and sesame oil in a food processor
  2. Stream reserved chickpeas liquid into the mixture as it blends until desired consistency is achieved.
